Mindful Mates

In Fall 2022, we designed a solution for people in relationships who may be feeling disconnected due to being long-distance, time restraints, or poor communication.

Our solution, Mindful Mates is a pair of palm-sized robots that connect to your mobile devices with which you can use to send emojis, colors, and voice notes to your partner's robot.
